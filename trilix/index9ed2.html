A new concept of mobility

Trilix was born from the meeting of functionality with design, which gives rise to a versatile, comfortable product with a strong stylistic component. The trellis frame fully reflects the objective of Moto Parilla, which is summed up in the search for the meeting point between what defines the world of motorcycles and the characteristic features of the bike universe. The technological choices make Trilix a perfect city e-bike, because it contains lightness, manageability and the possibility of being closed with a few simple moves. This does not preclude obtaining excellent performance, deriving from the powerful engine located in the center, capable of both perfectly balancing the bike and making every route, even the most difficult, within everyone’s reach.

Trilix can have different colors and moreover, similar to the big sister Carbon, it can be customized to get your own vehicle, different from all the others. There are two types of motor that can be mounted, 250W and 350W, ideal for every need.

Trilix VIP

The VIP version is even more functional, because thanks to Enviolo, an exclusive continuously variable gearbox, you will have the possibility to change the gear ratio between pedals and wheel in a single continuous movement. This will allow you to tackle the toughest routes with extreme simplicity.
