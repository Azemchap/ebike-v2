THE MOST EXCLUSIVE EBIKE IN THE WORLD

Carbon was born from an innovative project, which had the desire to melt the world of motorcycling and that of bicycles in a single product. Its unique shapes make it recognizable all over the world. The build quality resulting from the production process, completely Made in Italy, makes it a real work of art on wheels. Carbon can be used in any context, from urban to more sporty use, because its resistance and, at the same time, unparalleled elegance, make it suitable for any environment.

Carbon can have different colors, but nothing prevents you from creating a personalized design for those how are looking for further elegance or aggressiveness. Furthermore, there are four types of motor that can be mounted: 250W, 500W, 750W and 1000W. For those wishing to have even more power, it is possible to install even more performing engines.

Colors

CLUB

Club Edition is the perfect starting point to create the customization that best suits the customer's taste, the large surfaces of the carbon frame allow you to create illustrations and dedicated liveries with the support of our designers.

FACTORY ORANGE

Factory reflects the racing soul of Moto Parilla,
a dynamic identity born on the tracks it contrasts
shiny and opaque personifying the being of
rational and extreme pilot.

FORESTA

Foresta was born as the spearhead of carbon graphics as it brings us closer to nature, it has been studied by analyzing the natural textures that we experience every day. We want to enhance the values of life and the beautiful country that is Italy.

MISTERO

Mistero stands out for its iconicity and tradition, an over time livery that does not feel the passing of the years characterized by colors of the same chromatic range, perfect for any occasion, all with a matte finish for those who love to appear.

PASSIONE

Passione is the personification of a dream, stands out in the
world thanks to Moto Parilla red, different from any other color in terms of brightness and intensity, a harmonious symphony is born when the red takes possession of violet reflections.

PERFORMANCE

Performance shows the world who carbon is. An aggressive livery that highlights the dynamism of the frame by sinuously following every change of direction and tension of it. We want to enhance the aesthetic quality of the carbon fiber, leaving it breathtaking.

CREATE YOUR OWN CARBON

With the Tailor Made project, we make it possible to create your own Carbon from scratch. From the carbon fiber of the core frame and blades, to the aluminum of the frame and swingarm, down to every detail that makes up the bike, everything can be chosen by the customer.

In collaboration with our design team, we start with the idea and work our way up to the finished product, through careful painting done by the best painters in the world.

Collaborate with us

Turn it into reality
